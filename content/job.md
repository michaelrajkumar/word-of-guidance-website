## For Jobseekers

### Do you see someone skilled in their work? They will serve before kings; they will not serve before officials of low rank. (<a href="https://www.biblegateway.com/passage/?search=Proverbs+22:29&version=NIV" target="_blank" rel="noopener noreferrer">Proverbs 22:29</a>)

You may check the following popular websites frequently, or subscribe to some of them to receive updates daily or weekly. You can also upload your resume, so that potential employers can contact you. I have no connection with any of these popular websites, but I know them to be highly visited by countless job seekers every hour.
