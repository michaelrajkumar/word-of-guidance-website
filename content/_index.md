**Welcome to the Word of Guidance Website!**

This website offers spiritual resources meant to help individuals receive Peace from the Lord Jesus Christ—the Savior and the Light of the world, and the Prince of Peace.

**This website also provides FREE information on the following:** 

**Faith-based guidance for jobseekers:** Offering biblical insights on education, career, life, and family, especially through the teachings of the Lord Jesus Christ.

**Tele-Counseling for Professionals on Work and Life Balance:** Providing free counseling on authentic leadership, work-life balance, peaceful work and life relationships, and networking, with a focus on serving the local Church through voluntary service.

**Support through Personal Crises:** Offering biblical hope and prayer to help individuals navigate personal struggles.

**Advice on Christian marriage:** Offering Biblical principles for a happy married life and a heavenly Christian home.

This site also features resources such as free articles, audio and video messages on Christian living, guidance on overcoming life's challenges, and information on job vacancies, career development, networking opportunities and matrimonial links.
